About Grant English Academy

Professional Workplace English for Saudi Arabia and the Middle East

Our Mission

Practical English for Work

Grant English Academy is a Workplace English training platform designed for Arabic-speaking professionals and employees in Saudi Arabia. We focus solely on practical, real-world communication—meetings, introductions, customer service, and everyday workplace situations.
